# Organization Structure

## Company Overview
- **Type:** Mid-size Real Estate Tech Sales Company
- **Focus:** Real estate sales with heavy technology integration
- **Estimated Headcount:** 150-300 employees (~30-40% in tech roles)

---

## Executive Leadership

| Role | Reports To | Responsibility |
|------|------------|----------------|
| CEO | Board | Overall leadership and strategy |
| COO | CEO | Operations oversight |
| CFO | CEO | Finance and administration |
| CTO | CEO | Technology strategy and innovation |

---

## Division Structure

### 1. Technology & Engineering
**Head:** CTO

#### Engineering
- **VP of Engineering**
  - Backend Engineering Team
  - Frontend/Mobile Engineering Team
  - DevOps & Infrastructure Team
  - QA & Testing Team

#### Product
- **VP of Product**
  - Product Managers
  - UX/UI Design Team

#### Data & Analytics
- **Director of Data**
  - Data Engineers
  - Data Scientists/ML Engineers

---

### 2. Sales & Revenue
**Head:** VP of Sales (reports to CEO)

- **Regional Sales Directors** (by geography)
  - Sales Managers
    - Sales Representatives/Agents
- **Inside Sales Team**
- **Sales Operations & Enablement**

---

### 3. Marketing
**Head:** VP of Marketing (reports to CEO)

- Digital Marketing (SEO, SEM, Social)
- Content & Brand Team
- Lead Generation & Demand Gen
- Marketing Analytics

---

### 4. Real Estate Operations
**Head:** VP of Real Estate Operations (reports to COO)

- Property Listings Management
- Transaction Coordination
- Compliance & Legal Liaison
- Partner/Broker Relations

---

### 5. Customer Success
**Head:** VP of Customer Success (reports to COO)

- Customer Onboarding
- Account Management
- Customer Support

---

### 6. Corporate Functions
**Head:** COO / CFO (shared oversight)

- HR & People Operations
- Finance & Accounting
- Legal & Compliance
- Facilities/Admin

---

## Key Characteristics

1. **Tech-Heavy Structure:** ~30-40% of headcount in Technology & Engineering
2. **Sales-Driven:** Regional structure allows for geographic market focus
3. **Data-Centric:** Dedicated Data & Analytics team for insights and ML
4. **Customer Focus:** Separate Customer Success division for retention
5. **Scalable:** Structure supports growth from 150 to 300+ employees
