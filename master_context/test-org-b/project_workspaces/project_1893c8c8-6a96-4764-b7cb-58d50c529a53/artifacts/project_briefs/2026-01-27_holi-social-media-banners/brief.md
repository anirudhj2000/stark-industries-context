# Holi Social Media Banners

## Overview

A multi-platform social media campaign to boost brand awareness during the Holi festival season through vibrant, festive banner designs.

## Project Type

Marketing Campaign

## Problem Statement

Holi presents a key opportunity to connect with audiences through culturally relevant content. The brand needs eye-catching social media banners that resonate with the festive spirit while reinforcing brand presence across all major platforms.

## Goals

1. **Increase brand visibility** during the Holi festival period
2. **Create platform-optimized banners** for Instagram, Facebook, LinkedIn, and Twitter/X
3. **Deliver culturally resonant content** that connects with the target audience
4. **Maintain brand consistency** across all creative assets

## Target Audience

- Existing followers and customers across social media platforms
- Potential customers engaging with Holi-related content
- Professional network (LinkedIn) and casual social media users

## Deliverables

| Platform | Format | Dimensions |
|----------|--------|------------|
| Instagram | Feed Post | 1080 x 1080 px |
| Instagram | Story | 1080 x 1920 px |
| Facebook | Post | 1200 x 630 px |
| Facebook | Cover | 820 x 312 px |
| LinkedIn | Post | 1200 x 1200 px |
| Twitter/X | Post | 1600 x 900 px |

## Visual Direction

To be determined based on brand guidelines. Options include:
- Traditional & vibrant (classic Holi colors, cultural motifs)
- Modern & minimal (clean designs, subtle color splashes)
- Playful & fun (bold typography, energetic vibes)
- Brand-centric (Holi theme integrated with existing brand identity)

## Success Criteria

- Timely delivery before Holi festival
- Banners optimized for each platform's specifications
- Cohesive visual identity across all platforms
- Increased engagement metrics during the campaign period

## Constraints

- Must align with brand guidelines
- Delivery required before Holi 2026
- Content should be culturally appropriate and inclusive
