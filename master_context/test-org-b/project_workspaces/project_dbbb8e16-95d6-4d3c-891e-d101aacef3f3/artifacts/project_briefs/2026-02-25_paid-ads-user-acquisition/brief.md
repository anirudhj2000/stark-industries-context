# Project Brief: Paid Ads User Acquisition Campaign

**Date**: 2026-02-25
**Type**: MARKETING_CAMPAIGN
**Status**: Draft

## Overview

Launch a paid advertising campaign to drive new user acquisition for the portal. The initiative will span Google Ads, social media advertising, and retargeting campaigns, with a focus on maintaining cost-per-acquisition (CPA) efficiency while scaling spend.

## Problem Statement

The portal needs a scalable, predictable channel for acquiring new users. While organic growth is valuable, paid advertising provides the ability to control volume and target specific audiences. Before scaling, the team needs to define the Ideal Customer Profile (ICP) to ensure ad spend is directed at high-value prospects.

## Goals

- Define the Ideal Customer Profile (ICP) to guide targeting
- Launch paid campaigns across Google Ads, social platforms, and retargeting
- Establish a target CPA and optimize campaigns to stay under it
- Build a scalable acquisition engine that can grow with increased budget
- Generate learnings about which audiences, messages, and channels perform best
